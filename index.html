Haryanvi Jalebi started its journey in 2013, and with consistent hard work and dedication, they opened their first permanent shop in Dantewada. Initially, it was run by two brothers, Ramesh Jangid and Mahendra Jangid. Later, their younger brother, Foji Sahab Jangid, joined them, and together, they opened another shop in Kirandul in 2023, led by Ramesh Jangid. In October 2024, after proving their success, they expanded further by establishing Haryanvi Sweets. Today, after 11 years, they continue to serve authentic Haryanvi flavors with pride.
